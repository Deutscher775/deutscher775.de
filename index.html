PyLeaf - Custom Nanoleaf controller

PyLeaf is a custom Nanoleaf controller application that open-source and in development.
It's planned to support community-developed plugins, advanced schedules and integrations with other services.
To run the app you need to run my nanoleaf-api that is also open-source.

PyLeaf App: https://github.com/deutscher775/pyleaf
Nanoleaf API: https://github.com/deutscher775/nanoleaf-api

Astroid - The link between Discord, Guilded and Revolt.

Astroid is a multi-platform, open-source bridge in development.
It links you Discord, Guilded and Revolt (beta only) servers and bridges messages, attachments and embeddeds seemlesly between all platforms.
You can use our infrastructure or host the API, Bots or all on your own.

Github: https://github.com/astroid-app/v2
Website: https://astroid.cc
